Bringing it all together

We bring travel industry and functional programming together. We're using Haskell, a pure functional language, to make the development of correct, safe and modern software systems possible.

With our platform we are able to built systems that are

  • making financial and sensitive operations safe and robust
  • making the most complex flows manageable
  • able to apply real-time contextual signals and preference filters
  • able to match workflows to percipients
